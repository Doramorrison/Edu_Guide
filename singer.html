SINGER

Music (IGCSE Music)

This subject is fundamental for any musician, focusing on developing skills in music theory, composition, and performance, and is essential for those looking to pursue music professionally.

English Language

These subjects help enhance writing and communication skills, enabling musicians to articulate their ideas, analyze music history, and create effective written content about music.

Mathematics

Mathematics plays a significant role in music, helping with understanding rhythm, timing, and patterns, as well as key music theory concepts like time signatures and scales.

Physics

Physics offers valuable knowledge of sound, acoustics, and the mechanics of musical instruments, all of which are important for music production and sound engineering.

Art

Art nurtures creativity and visual expression, providing essential skills for designing album covers, creating music videos, and contributing to the visual elements of live performances.

Drama

Drama develops confidence, stage presence, and performance skills, all of which are essential for delivering impactful live music performances and connecting with an audience.

Top 3 Easiest Countries to Apply for scholarship

IN ASIA

South Korea
Japan
Singapore

Why It's easy?

South Korea : Strong government and institutional support for arts make scholarships accessible. Programs blend Korean traditions with global music styles, and universities like Korea National University of Arts focus on nurturing international talent.

Japan : Offers a unique fusion of traditional and modern music education. Scholarships target international students who engage with its rich cultural heritage, and top-tier schools make applications streamlined.

Singapore : Known for its cultural diversity, it hosts high-quality music conservatories with substantial financial aid and clear scholarship processes for foreign students.
